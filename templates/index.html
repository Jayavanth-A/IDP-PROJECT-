🔬 About This Model

A Random Forest Classifier (300 trees) trained on clinical patient records. Class imbalance is handled with SMOTE, and features are standardised via StandardScaler. The model predicts whether a patient will be readmitted within 30 days of discharge.

    42%
  • Diagnosis Code
    36%

⚠️ Risk Interpretation

High Risk (>50%)
Patient likely to be readmitted. Recommend enhanced discharge planning, follow-up scheduling, and medication reconciliation.

Low Risk (≤50%)
Standard discharge protocol. Monitor for changes in clinical status before discharge.
